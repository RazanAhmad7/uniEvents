About Our Project

The Events University at Jordan University of Science and Technology (JUST) is a specialized platform that caters to the diverse academic and extracurricular interests of its community, with a focus on key colleges like Information Technology (IT), Medicine, and Engineering.

The platform is designed to foster collaboration, innovation, and networking through a variety of events, including conferences, workshops, hackathons, medical symposia, and engineering exhibitions.
