The Line Tracing Robotics Competition TCRT 2024

Location : NB99
Date : 22 Oct 2024
Time : 10:00 AM - 2:00 PM

The Line Tracing Robotics Competition TCRT 2024 was held at Jordan University of Science and Technology (JUST), featuring over 35 participating teams. Organized by the Deanship of Student Affairs and the Computer and Information Club (CTC), the event aimed to promote innovation, teamwork, and leadership among students. Teams were tasked with designing and programming robots to autonomously navigate a line-marked track. The competition included multiple rounds to test speed, accuracy, and adaptability. Participants demonstrated their creativity and problem-solving skills while collaborating and sharing ideas. Awards were presented for categories such as Best Design and Overall Champion, recognizing outstanding performances. The event concluded with a ceremony celebrating the achievements of the participants and inspiring them to pursue careers in STEM fields.
